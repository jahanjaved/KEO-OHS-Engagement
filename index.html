Key Message

Three things we must get right

01

Start First, Correct Later Must Stop

Work shall not proceed until the required controls are verified at the work front. Unsafe conditions must be stopped before the task continues.

02

Scaffolding and Work at Height Control

Scaffolds, platforms and temporary arrangements must have proper inspection, approval, access, edge protection and design control before use.

03

Summer Readiness and Workforce Care

Contractors must maintain hydration, welfare arrangements, supervision, heat stress controls and clear communication to protect the workforce.

Required Site Behaviour

Contractor and subcontractor expectations

Every supervisor, engineer and HSE representative must verify site conditions before allowing work to proceed. The message is simple: no shortcut, no unsafe start, no acceptance of repeated non-compliance.

  • Verify PTW, access, barricading and supervision before work starts.
  • Stop unsafe work immediately and escalate high-risk conditions.
  • Maintain scaffolding, edge protection and temporary works controls.
  • Brief subcontractor workforce on weekly critical safety expectations.
  • Submit closeout evidence and confirm similar locations are checked.

Safety is a shared responsibility. Plan the work, verify the controls, and protect the workforce.
